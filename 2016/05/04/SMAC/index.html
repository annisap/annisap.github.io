SMAC computing platform transforms IT investments

The impressive growth of social networking services has made available an unprecedented scale of personal data, social relationships and behaviors that when are measured, mined and interpreted are of an enormous value. In this post you will find out about the third computing platform, known as SMAC.

How much is your personal data worth?

Personal data is the new currency

Many research articles identify the arrival of Big Data era in the advent of social networking data, IOT, and cloud. The first two, are generating large volumes of data in a great velocity that have different structures and formats. Amazon’s Echo, a voice command device, is the latest futuristic IoT trend that can hear you even while music is playing and can be used from answering questions about news and traffic to controlling your home functioning such as turning on/off lights and thermostats. If you are an IoT enthusiastic you can try building your own following some simple steps. Although, IoT isn’t producing a data deluge yet, by 2020 they will reach billions as Cisco and IDC estimate.

Image: Cisco

The third one, Cloud Computing, is the powerful technology that make affordable big data and IoT. If you are a cloud developer, check the brand new Azure (CC platform) starter kits that Microsoft announced in it’s Build 2016 event.
To get to the point, the synergy of the above three technologies together with mobile computing make prominent the third computing platform known as SMAC (Social, Mobile, Analytics & big data, Cloud). SMAC change how business develop and promote products, engage with customers, enhance collaboration with partners and other, compete, and succeed. Social stands for the wide adoption of social networks either these are of generic-purposes like Facebook or they are of business purposes like yammer. Mobile stands for the device that mostly people use during their day for entertainment, shopping and communication among other things. Big Data and analytics like web analytics, social media sentiment analysis, predictive analytics and video analytics have become standard investments in enterprises, so as to power real-time marketing. Generally, using these technologies wisely would create competitive advantage.

Image: http://www.collaborative.com/

Cloud , social media, mobile computing and big data analytics are four emerging technologies that offer new opportunities where IT departments should stay focus and create innovative ideas and entrepreneurs should adapt their business models in this current infrastructure. Also in the science stream, researchers invest ideas on these four topics.
